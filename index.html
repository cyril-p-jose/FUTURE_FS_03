About Harbor Brew Café

Nestled beside Kerala's serene backwaters, Harbor Brew Café is a destination where nature, comfort, and great coffee meet.

Surrounded by coconut trees, riverside seating, traditional fishing boats, and stunning sunset views, we offer a unique café experience inspired by Kerala's rich coastal culture.

Whether you're enjoying a peaceful morning coffee, working remotely, or spending time with friends, Harbor Brew Café provides the perfect atmosphere.
